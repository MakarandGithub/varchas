Soch

"soch - Think because you can change"

Soch works for creating awareness about the social problems prevailing in the society. Our main aim is to educate people about social issues and problems by various effective and innovative approaches. We work with the motto "Sometimes little things show big effect".

Every year, we focus on a problem and try to find a solution for it. We talk directly to general public and get to know the exact problem from their point of view, thereby effectively understanding it in a far better way. We call specialists in that field and conduct an open channel discussion panel to try and arrive at a solution that is most logical and appreciated by the mass. By doing this, we get a solution that can effectively deal with that problem. This year we chose the topic "SPORTS EDUCATION".
In regard with this we are organizing an inter school drama competition and more.
