Athletics

Rules:

  1. Maximum 3 participant per event will be accepted from each college.
  2. An individual can participate in maximum 5 events (3 track & 2 field) excluding relays.
  3. Number of Heats will be decided on the spot based on the number of participants.
  4. The rules will be considered according to IAAF.
  5. An event may stand cancelled in case of insufficient participation.
